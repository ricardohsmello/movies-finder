The Purpose of Movie Finder

Ever spent 30 minutes browsing only to give up on picking a movie? That’s why I built Movie Finder — to cut through indecision and offer suggestions based on how you feel, not just titles or actors.

Just type something like “a movie about pyramids, ancient Egypt, and a spaceship” or “the greatest World Cup champions of all time”, and let the app serve you semantically relevant results.

Under the Hood

Movie Finder is a lightweight frontend backed by a powerful stack: Quarkus for performance and startup speed, and MongoDB Atlas Vector Search for understanding natural language inputs using vector embeddings.

The UI is written in plain HTML/CSS with minimal JS, and the app is deployed on Render using a custom Dockerfile that packages the Quarkus backend as a container. This setup ensures fast and reproducible deployments with minimal configuration.

You can check out the source code on Github. and even contribute if you'd like.
